Pizza Dough

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup of warm water
  • 1 TBSP Active Dry Yeast
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 TBSP olive oil
  • 2 1/2 cups of bread flour
Directions
  1. In a large bowl mix the flour, salt and yeast
  2. Incorporate the warm water oil and
  3. Mix until a shaggy dough comes together
  4. Turn onto a floured surface and knead for two minutes
  5. Place kneaded dough into a greased bowl
  6. Cover dough and let rest for 30 minutes
  7. After 30 mionutes, punch dough down
  8. Remove dough from bowl and form into a ball
