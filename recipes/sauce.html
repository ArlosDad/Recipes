Pizza Sauce

Ingredients:
  • One 28oz can of whole peeled tomatoes
  • Two finely grated garlic cloves
  • Two TBSP olive oil
  • One and one half tsp fine sea salt
    • Directions:
    1. Hand crush tomatoes until sauce like consistency
    2. whisk oil, garlic and salt into the tomatoes
    3. Cover and chill for three hours before using
